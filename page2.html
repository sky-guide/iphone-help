Your Sky+HD box needs to be connected to the internet and once it is you'll also have access to a huge library of TV and movies on demand. There are two ways of setting this connection up:

Wireless connection

This is recommended if your Sky+HD box and broadband router are in different rooms or if you prefer less clutter.

To get started you'll need the Wireless Connector, which you can buy from our accessories website. Then follow the step by step guide in the packaging to connect your Sky+HD box to your broadband network wirelessly.

Wired connection

This is recommended if your Sky+HD box and broadband router are close together.

To get started you'll need an Ethernet cable, push one end of the cable into a spare Ethernet port on your broadband router and the other end into the Ethernet port on the back of your Sky+HD box. Once this is done you'll see a message on your TV instructing you to press select on your Sky remote control and follow the on screen instructions.
