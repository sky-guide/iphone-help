Help > How to use

Connect your iPhone to the same Wi-Fi network as your Sky+HD box, and the app will search for any compatible Sky+HD boxes on the network. Once the Sky+ app has found one, it'll connect and you're good to go.

Navigation bar

If you tap the Sky logo at the top of the Sky+ app for iPad, a navigation bar will appear and you'll be able to access the different sections of the app. The Planner section will only work when your iPad is connected to your Sky+HD box via your wifi network.

TV Guide

In the TV Guide on the Sky+ app for iPhone, you can find out what's on over the next seven days. You'll also be able to sort programmes by genre, easily find the schedule for a chosen day, and press a channel logo to watch that channel on your TV.

Programme detail guide

Once you've found the programme you are looking for, you'll see the summary of that programme and the option to either Record or Remind. If the show is being broadcast at that time, you'll also be able to press watch and the programme will start playing on your TV.

Using your iPhone as a remote control

To use your iPhone as a remote control using the Sky+ app, tap the small circular icon in the top right hand corner of the screen. To find out the correct finger movements to operate the iPhone for use as a remote control, select Show Gesture Guide and the instructions will appear.

The Planner

Here you'll see all the recordings you have in your Planner, as well as the future recordings and reminders you have set. Just tap on a tab to sort your planner by that genre and tap it again to change the date order.

Once you've found a programme you want, tap on it and the Planner detail page will appear giving you the option to start playing it on your TV.

Settings

In the Sky+HD box connectivity section of the Sky+ app for iPhone, you'll be able to choose which box to connect to (as long as it's also got the new Sky Guide and it's connected to the same broadband network).

You can also turn off the new features and stop the app connecting to the Sky+HD box.
